# 🚀 LinkedIn Posting Guide: CompareWise AI

Sharing your project on LinkedIn is a great way to showcase your skills to recruiters and peers. Here are two templates and some tips to make your post stand out.

---

## 📸 Media Tips (Crucial!)
Posts with visuals get **3x more engagement**. Before posting:
1.  **Record a 30-second clip:** Show yourself dragging a few PDFs into the app and the AI generating the table.
2.  **Screenshot the Results:** Use a high-quality screenshot of the Comparison Table and the AI Recommendation.
3.  **GitHub Link:** Make sure your GitHub repository is public!

---

## 💡 Pro-Tips for Posting:
1.  **Tag Ollama:** They often repost community projects!
2.  **The First Comment:** Post your GitHub link in the **first comment** rather than the post body (LinkedIn's algorithm sometimes prefers this to keep users on their site).
3.  **Engage:** If people comment, reply to them! It helps the post reach more people.
